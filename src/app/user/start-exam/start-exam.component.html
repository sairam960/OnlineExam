Instructions for Technical Test

The clock will be at the server. The countdown timer at the right corner of the screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the examination. When the timer reaches zero the examination ends by itself. You need to terminate the examination or submit the paper.

The question palette displayed on the right side of the screen will show the status of each question.

Navigating to a Question

To navigate to a Question:
Select an answer from the 4 options available and click on Next to go to next question.
You can revisit previous questions by Previous button.
Click on Finish to finish your test.

Answering a Question

To select your answer, click on the button of one of the options.
To deselect your answer, click on the button of chosen option again.
To change your chosen answer, click on the button of another option.
To save your answer, you must click on Save & Next button.

Weightage of Questions

This paper consists a total of 10 questions.
Each question is of 1 mark and Weightage of paper is for 10 marks.
For each right answer you are given 1 mark and there are no negative marks for the wrong answers.
No marks are awareded for the un-answered questions.
